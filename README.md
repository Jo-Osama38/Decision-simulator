# Decision Simulator 🎮📚💼🧟

**Decision Simulator** is an interactive text-based game where your choices shape the story. You can select different modes and each decision affects your personal stats such as Energy, Study, Friendship, and Balance.

---

## 📌 Features

- Four different game modes:  
  1. **High School Mode** 📚 – Experience student life  
  2. **Work Mode** 💼 – Experience work life  
  3. **Zombie Mode** 🧟 – Survive the zombie apocalypse  
  4. **Gamer Mode** 🎮 – Compete in gaming tournaments  

- **Multiple decisions** that affect your stats:  
  - 📚 Study  
  - ⚡ Energy  
  - 👥 Friendship  
  - ⚖️ Balance

- **Multiple endings** depending on your choices  
- **Secret endings** when special conditions are met  
- Attractive UI with text effects and gradient colors
